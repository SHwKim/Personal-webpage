About

Genome Editing, Precision Medicine, Bridging Academia and Industry

I’m passionate about developing innovative gene therapy and interested in joining a cohesive research team working on genome editing.

I really enjoy working on cutting-edge therapeutic genome editing tools such as RNAi, CRISPR/Cas9, CRISPR/Cas13, Base editing, Prime editing, and ADAR.

With diverse research experiences based on molecular biology along with my undergraduate background in business, I have cemented my aim to seek further education in the most promising field, targeted gene therapy. I want to contribute my knowledge and passion to the development of gene therapy.

After receiving my Ph.D., I am eager to find an appropriate position at global pharmaceutical companies that will allow me to conduct extensive independent research to understand genetic predisposition in order to develop novel precision medicine. I am ready for this journey to begin now.

Outside of lab: I enjoy going to the musical theater and also love to travel for exceptional cuisine.
